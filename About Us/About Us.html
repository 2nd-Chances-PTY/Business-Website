Mission

What do we do today?

  • Provide a platform which will strive at making our members dreams a reality. Whether it be through our free or paid services we just want to see the members of our community grow.

    • Who do we serve?

  • People who need a 2nd chance at life whether it be physically, mentally or financially.

    • What are we trying to accomplish?

  • To help people achieve their dreams. Whether it be having a second income, starting a business or getting their dream body or property. We look at the saying that goes "too many cooks spoils the broth" and apply it to life.

    • What impact do we want to achieve?

  • We simply want to see our clients living a better quality of life.

    • Values

    What do we stand for?

  • Honesty. A sales person knows how to sell products and services but a great sales person is honest about what he/she is selling.

    • What behaviors do we value over all else?

  • Honesty,Strong integrity and amazing work ethic.

    • How will we conduct our activities to achieve our mission and vision?

  • We will strive to give the customer what they want by listening more than speaking simply because when you're speaking, you're not listening and doing our best to do what we're asked to do to help our community reach their goals.

    • How do we treat members of our own organization and community?

  • With respect at all times. We understand that even though there may be times where we come across unruly members. We believe that, that is a reflection of them and not us so we shall simply lead by showing respect regardless.


    • Vision

    Where are we going moving forward?

  • We plan on creating equal opportunity for people to achieve their dreams especially after the pandemic and the current state it has left the majority of us in.

    • What do we want to achieve in the future?

  • Provide a platform not only online but in person for people to be able to start learning skills that would help them lead healthier lives or like what inspired the name of our comapany, give them a second chance at creating the life they have always dreamed of living regardless of where they come from.

    • What kind of future society do we envision?

  • A healthier society. Not only healthier when it comes to finances but, both mentally and physically too.
